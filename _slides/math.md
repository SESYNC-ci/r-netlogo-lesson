## Basic math 

R had a lot of built-in mathematical functionality. Use common operators such as `+`,`-`,`*`,`/`,`( )`,`^` and names like `pi`,`max`,`range`,`sin`,`log` and `sqrt` to use R for calculations.


~~~r
1 + 2
~~~
{:.input}

~~~
[1] 3
~~~
{:.output}


~~~r
5/3
~~~
{:.input}

~~~
[1] 1.666667
~~~
{:.output}


~~~r
16/(sqrt(4))
~~~
{:.input}

~~~
[1] 8
~~~
{:.output}
